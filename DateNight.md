## Activity Details

### Budget-Friendly Options (Free - £15pp)

**[Manchester Art Gallery](https://manchesterartgallery.org/)**
- Over 25,000 objects including Pre-Raphaelite masterpieces
- Open: Tue-Sun, 10am-5pm (last entry 4:30pm)
- Nearest Metrolink: St Peter's Square

**[The Lowry Gallery](https://thelowry.com/)**
- Features L.S. Lowry paintings and contemporary exhibitions
- Free gallery admission; theatre shows vary (£12-£38)
- Located at Salford Quays waterfront

**[NQ64 Arcade Bar](https://nq64.co.uk/manchester-northern-quarter/)**
- Retro gaming with 40+ arcade machines and pinball
- Tokens: £9.50 for 15 or £13 for 25
- Consoles are free to play; 18+ only

### Mid-Range Options (£20-£35pp)

**[Electric Shuffle](https://electricshuffle.com/uk/manchester)**
- Shuffleboard with cocktails and pizza
- Bottomless brunch: £30pp (includes 2hrs pizza, prosecco, 1hr shuffleboard)
- Open until 1am on Saturdays

**[Escape Room (Breakout Manchester)](https://breakoutmanchester.com/)**
- 60-minute puzzle challenges
- Themes include murder mystery, bank heist, prison escape
- Book in advance recommended

**[Pottery Painting (Brooklyn Pottery)](https://www.brooklynpottery.co.uk/)**
- Relaxed creative session decorating ceramics
- Brooklyn Pottery in Stockport offers cosy atmosphere with refreshments
- No experience needed

### Active Options (£25-£30pp)

**[The Snow Centre Manchester](https://thesnowcentre.com/manchester/)**
- UK's longest indoor snow slope
- 1hr: £28pp, 2hr: £33pp (includes equipment hire)
- Beginner-friendly with lessons available

**Bowling**
- [Lane7 (Deansgate)](https://lane7.com/venue/manchester-deansgate/): £10.50pp with games bar atmosphere
- [Hollywood Bowl (Ashton)](https://www.hollywoodbowl.co.uk/manchester): From £9.50pp, early booking discounts available
